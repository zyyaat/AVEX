What this fixture exercises

Dark depth through luminance steps.

Three-step depth

Blurple accent

gg sans geometry

Friendly rounded terminals on a/g/s. Weight 400/500/600/700/800 — hierarchy through contrast, not color. 16px body never shrinks.
