Product areas

What this fixture exercises

Variable precision

Weight stops 320, 330, 340, 450, 480, 540, 700 — the granular control IS the design. Hierarchy through micro-differences.

Monochrome chrome

#000000 and #ffffff only. Color lives in product content — gradients, screenshots, and embedded designs — never the shell.

Dashed focus

2px dashed outline on all interactive elements — echoes selection handles in the Figma editor. The website speaks the product's UI.

Actions

All buttons use pill (50px) radius.

No sharp corners on interactive elements. Black solid for primary; bordered white for secondary. Focus is dashed, never solid.
